About the Culinary Arts Team

The Augusta Technical College Culinary Arts Team is dedicated to providing high-quality food service to our campus community. Our team of trained culinary professionals brings years of experience and passion for excellence to every meal we prepare.

Our Mission

We are committed to preparing delicious, nutritious meals using quality ingredients while providing students with practical experience in food preparation and service.

What We Offer

Our to-go food service provides convenient, affordable meals for students, faculty, and staff. Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a special catered event, we're here to serve you with professionalism and quality.

Get Involved

Interested in learning more about our culinary programs? Contact us today to find out how you can join our team or participate in special events!
